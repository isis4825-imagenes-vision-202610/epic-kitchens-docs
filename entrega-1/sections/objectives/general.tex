\section{General Objective}

To develop and evaluate a deep learning model for multimodal video-text retrieval using the \href{https://epic-kitchens.github.io/}{\textit{EPIC-KITCHENS-100}} dataset, capable of identifying shared semantic representations that align egocentric video segments and textual descriptions, thereby generating relevance rankings for both video-to-text and text-to-video retrieval tasks.
